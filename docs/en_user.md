# SMAASH Web — User Guide (English)

## Overview

SMAASH Web is the web portal for the SMAASH game platform. It provides user accounts, a leaderboard, a gallery, a webstore for in-game items, news, and game releases — all accessible from a browser.

---

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Open the app and click **Sign up** on the login page.
2. Enter your email address and choose a password (minimum 8 characters).
3. Confirm your password in the second field.
4. Click **Create Account**.
5. On success you are redirected to the login page. Sign in with your new credentials.

> If registration fails, check that both password fields match and that your email is not already in use. Try again — the form resets itself cleanly on each attempt.

### Logging In

1. Enter your email and password on the login screen.
2. Click **Login**.
3. On success you are taken to the app.

### Logging Out

Click your username in the top-right corner to open the account menu, then select **Log out**. On mobile, open the hamburger menu (top-left) and scroll to the bottom of the drawer.

---

## Navigation

The top navigation bar contains:

| Item | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Leaderboard** | Ranked list of top players |
| **Gallery** | Browse game screenshots and art |
| **Releases** | Game version history and patch notes |
| **Webstore** | Purchase in-game items with coins |
| **News** | Latest announcements |

On mobile all navigation items are inside the hamburger (☰) drawer.

---

## Pages

### Leaderboard

Shows the highest-ranked players by wins. You can see each player's display name, coin balance, win count, and match count.

### Gallery

A visual gallery of game content — characters, levels, and screenshots. Use the filters to browse by category or rarity.

### Releases

A list of all released game versions with changelog notes and download links sourced directly from the project repository.

### Webstore

Browse and purchase in-game items using your coin balance. Items are shown with name, rarity (Common → Legendary), categories, and price. Click an item to see details.

### News

Latest news posts from the development team.

---

## Profile

### Viewing Your Profile

Click your username in the navbar to go to your profile. Your profile shows your display name, coin balance, and match history.

### Switching Profiles

A single account can have multiple player profiles. Click **Change Profile** in the account menu to create or switch profiles.

### Settings

Access **Settings** from the account menu to configure:

- **Dark Mode** — switches the colour scheme to dark.
- **Liquid Glass** — enables a frosted-glass visual style.
- **Animations** — toggles entrance and transition animations.
- **Language** — switches the UI between English and Hungarian.

---

## Appearance

The app supports two visual modes:

- **Standard** — solid backgrounds with accent colours.
- **Liquid Glass** — translucent, frosted glass surfaces.

Both modes support **Light** and **Dark** variants. All settings are saved in your browser and persist across sessions.

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Sign up says "Captcha verification failed" | This is a known issue on some mobile browsers. Refresh the page and try again. The app now continues registration even if the captcha check cannot run. |
| "User already exists" on signup | The email address is already registered. Use a different email or log in. |
| Login fails with correct credentials | Check for typos. If the account was banned, you will see a notification. |
| Page appears blank | Hard-refresh the browser (Ctrl+Shift+R / Cmd+Shift+R). |
| Navbar looks cramped | Resize or rotate your device — the layout adapts to different screen sizes. |
